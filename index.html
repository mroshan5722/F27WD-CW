To participate in all our travels, you need to bring

  1. Valid passport issued by the police (driving license not enough)
  2. Valid "Försäkringskassan's EU card "
  3. Travel insurance with an insurance company
  4. "Vaccine passport" or similar supporting documents
  5. Estonians want to stop infection already at the border. Therefore, from 1/12, you must send a notification of your arrival to Estonia no earlier than 3 days before. This is an assurance that you have no Covid symptoms.

Go to Terviseamet to fill in a form that you get from us. This applies until further notice
