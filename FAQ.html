Common Questions

When will the bills come?

The invoice usually arrives between 4 to 3 weeks before departure. We do not send out a partial payment, but the entire invoice is sent at once. It can also be sent out a little earlier if the trip is fully booked. The invoice also includes a travel program and the address at the time of booking.

Unpaid bill is not considered a cancellation but the cancellation is not completed until you have received a cancellation notice from our office (see Travel conditions) Invoice will not be sent out if we see that the number of participants is too few (less than 10 people) of too few participants, the message will be sent no later than 2 weeks before the planned departure to the telephone number you provided when booking

Can the trip be cancelled?

Should the trip be canceled, Feel free to contact me if the bill and information has not reached you about 10 days before departure.Then you may have time to book another trip or move to another departure date that we know will go

The bill will not be sent until we see that the trip is canceled, so you pay nothing until the bill and travel program comes to you. If the bill arrives earlier, the departure is guaranteed the shipping company can charter out the entire ship at short notice, which means that the trip is canceled due to this.

Covid-19 and infection information

The pandemic seems to be under some sort of control. Many are vaccinated, even in Estonia, and people are getting vaccinated more and more.

Most have received a paper or receipt for the vaccination, you can also order a so-called "covid pass" on your mobile phone. Right now (end of November) there are controls because it is feared to increase. It is counted in the number of infected 100,000 people. If the number exceeds 75 in the country where you come from, you are required to have proof of vaccination or that you have had the infection earlier within 6 months. But bring these papers or bring home a fresh covid passport on the phone. You can come to Estonia without these documents. Be sure to hand hygiene cough or sneeze into the armpit and keep the social distance as far as possible, even if you are vaccinated. Keep in mind that there are other infections as well.
