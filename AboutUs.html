About us 🤔


We have had trips to the Baltics since 1997. The bus business was founded in 1988. We have two buses.

Over the years, we have had tourist trips to Norway, Denmark, Finland, Russia, Estonia Latvia, Lithuania, Poland, Germany, Austria, France and Spain.As well as group trips to the USA, both east and west coast But now we focus mostly on Estonia.

We hold an International Traffic Permit , Travel Guarantee, F-tax and have the highest Creditworthiness.

We envision a world in which every person can fulfill their need for travel. A world where you can easily visit family and friends, access education and economic opportunities, and explore the world. As humans, we have the urge to explore. By traveling, you can be closer to the people you love. You can also discover more about yourself and the world around you. By empowering travelers through technology, Busbud aims to create a more accessible world for everyone, by offering access to sustainable transportation.
